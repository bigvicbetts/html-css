Clue

Clue is a movie based on the boardgame of the same name. It was released in 1985 and stars, among others, Tim Curry, Madeline Kahn, Christopher Lloyd, and Martin Mull. The movie takes place at a dinner party where the guests, who are all being blackmailed, find out who is blackmailing them. When the blackmailer is murdered, everyone is a suspect. Who dun' it?

Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure

Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure was released in 1989 and stars Keanu Reeves, Alex Winter, and George Carlin. It chronicles the adventures of two academically-challenged high school students who make use of a time-travelling phone booth to capture important historical figures and take them back to 1989 to help them with their history report.

Oscar

Oscar was released in 1991. It stars Sylvester Stallone and Tim Curry. It tells the story of a gangster who is trying to fulfill his father's dying wish that he become an honest man. On the day that he is to begin his new, law-abiding career, he is confronted with numerous calamaties which he must overcome.

The Princess Bride

The Princess Bride was released in 1987 and stars Cary Elwes, Mandy Patinkin, Peter Falk, Fred Savage, Andre the Giant and Billy Crystal. The main story is a fairy tale about a beautiful woman who was separated from her one true love and the lengths that he goes to in order to get back to her. The fairy tale is actually being read by a grandfather to his sick grandson.

Monty Python and the Holy Grail

Monty Python and the Holy Grail was released in 1975 and stars all of the member of the Monty Python comedy troupe. It tells the story of how King Arthur and his knights of Camelot accepted the quest, given to them by God, to find the Holy Grail. In typical Monty Python style, the knights are confronted by numerous insane obsticles which they must overcome before, eventually, getting arrested in a modern-day murder investigation.

The Jungle Book

The Jungle Book was released in 1942 and is based on the book written by Rudyard Kipling of the same name. It tells the tale of Mowgli, a child whose father was killed by a tiger while they were in the forest. Having no person to care for him, Mowgli is adopted into a wolf pack and befriends numerous other animals as well. Eventually he is discovered and returned to his mother, but he must learn human customs in order to survive.
