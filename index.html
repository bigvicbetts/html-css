Early Life

I was born in Detroit Lakes, MN on Dec. 5, 1981. I have five brother and four sisters. I am the second youngest of my siblings (I have one younger sister).

An interesting fact is that I actually share my birthday with my next older sister as well as my younger sister. I am exactly two years younger than my next older sister and three years older than my younger sister.
When I was less than a year old, my family moved from Minnessota to Chattanooga, TN where I was raised. I attended Tennessee Temple Academy from second grade until graduating high school in 2000.

College And Beyond

After graduating high school, I went on to college at UT Chattanooga. While visiting a friend at Texas A&M, though, I met the love of my life. After making the 26 hour round-trip from Chattanooga to College Station four times in the span of six months just to see her, I transferred to UT Arlington so pursue our relationship. On August 7, 2004 we got married at her church in Arlington. While we have certainly had our ups and downs, we have weathered every storm we've come across and now have three amazing children to help brighten our days even more.
